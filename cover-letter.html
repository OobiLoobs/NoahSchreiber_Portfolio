Cover Letter

I'm a software engineer who cares about building well‑designed systems and understanding how they work behind the scenes. As much as I emphasize the technical aspect of my work, I find the most satisfaction in creatively solving complex issues. I like thinking through how pieces fit together, where things can break, and how small decisions alter a system's behavior over time.

I'm intrigued by technical concepts and the challenge of simplifying them into understandable practices. I tend to approach problems by stepping back first, getting a feel for the situation as a whole, and then focusing on the specifics. By doing this, I reliably avoid quick fixes that will create bigger issues later.

Outside of development, my passion for video games, music, and digital art has expanded my technical toolkit. Those spaces reward curiosity, iteration, and attention to detail, all of which have made me more thoughtful about the design and implementation of user experiences. I see creativity as an integral part of the engineering process. It's a tool that helps me notice patterns, maintain flexibility, and solve problems when the obvious solution isn't necessarily the right one.

I work well with others and take pride in being a friendly face to anyone I come across. I believe that hard work and collaboration lead to success, so I trade competition for encouragement. I strive to be conscientious, reliable, and intentional with the projects I contribute to.

I'm most interested in an engineering role that values deep technical knowledge while encouraging creativity in problem-solving. I want to continue learning, build software that stands the test of time, and contribute in a way that's both valuable and meaningful.

Thanks for taking the time to read,

Noah Schreiber
